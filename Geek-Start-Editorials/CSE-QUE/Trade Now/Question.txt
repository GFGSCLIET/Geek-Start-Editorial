Dogecoin is a cryptocurrency created by software engineers Billy Markus and Jackson Palmer, who decided to create a payment system as a "joke", making fun of the wild speculation in cryptocurrencies at the time. Now its become the best source of passive income.

Elon wants to make a High Profit. He wants to maximize the profit by choosing a single day to buy one lot and choosing a different day in the future to sell that one lot.

You are given a list L daily prices where L[i] is the price of a dogecoin on an ith day.

Help Elon to have the maximum profit. Print the maximum profit he can achieve from this transaction. If he cannot achieve any profit, Print 0.

Input Format

the 1st line consists of Number of items.
the 1st line consists of list of prices seperated by white spaces.
Constraints

1 <= prices.length <= 10^5
0 <= prices[i] <= 10^4
Output Format

Print the maximum profit you can achieve from this transaction. If you cannot achieve any profit, Print 0.

Sample Input 0

6
7 1 5 3 6 4
Sample Output 0

5
Explanation 0

Buy on day 2 (price = 1) and sell on day 5 (price = 6), profit = 6-1 = 5.

Sample Input 1

5
7 6 4 3 1
Sample Output 1
0

Explanation 1

In this case, no transactions are done and the max profit = 0.

A man has forgotten his locker password. all he remembers is, that his password has 1 to n unique numbers. he managed to get all the numbers except the last digit. help him to unlock it.

Input Format

the 1st line consists of length of the actual password. The 2nd line consists array of n-1 digits, seperated by white spaces.

Constraints

1 <= n <= 9
Output Format

print the digit which is missing from the passowrd.

Sample Input 0

9
7 5 4 1 2 3 9 8
Sample Output 0

6
Explanation 0

here, 9 digit password., all digits are present except 6. hence, 6 is the required last digit.

Sample Input 1

6
2 4 6 1 3
Sample Output 1

5
Explanation 1

since 5 the missing digit, 5 is the required number.
